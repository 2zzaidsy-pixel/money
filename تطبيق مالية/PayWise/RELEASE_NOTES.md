# PayWise v1.0.0 — Release Notes

## What's New
- **Dashboard** — Real-time financial health score, survival date projection, monthly prediction, money leak detection, and quick-access shortcuts
- **Expense Tracking** — Log, categorize, filter, search, and edit expenses with beautiful charts
- **Smart Budgets** — Create category budgets with animated progress bars and real-time exceeded alerts
- **Financial Reports** — Period-based summaries with category breakdowns, health score timeline, and actionable insights
- **Savings Goals** — Set financial goals with animated progress tracking and milestone celebrations
- **Emergency Fund** — Track your safety net with coverage gauge and target month calculator
- **Simulator** — "What-if" scenarios (5 presets + custom) to see how changes in income/expenses affect your health
- **Subscription Manager** — Track recurring subscriptions, toggle active/inactive, see total monthly costs
- **AI-Powered Insights** — Health score algorithm, money leak detection, and personalized financial suggestions
- **Premium** — Unlock advanced reports, priority support, and unlimited goals

## Security & Privacy
- Bank-grade encryption for all sensitive data (EncryptedSharedPreferences)
- Secure authentication with Email, Google Sign-In, or Guest mode
- Full Privacy Policy and Terms of Service included in-app
- One-tap account deletion with data wipe

## Export & Backup
- Export reports as PDF or CSV
- Share via any app (email, messaging, cloud)
- Automatic cloud sync via Firebase (optional)

## Localization
- Full English and Arabic support
- Right-to-Left (RTL) layout for Arabic
- Multi-currency support (SAR, USD, EUR, GBP, AED, QAR, KWD, BHD)

## Technical
- Target: Android 8.0+ (API 26)
- Optimized for all screen sizes and orientations
- Dark mode support
- Accessibility-ready
- Crash reporting and performance monitoring via Firebase
